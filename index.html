Bio

I was born in Riverton, Utah as the youngest of five boys. I moved to Naperville, Illinois when I was 3, and moved back to Utah when I was 15. Throughout high school, I competed in DECA (ultimately placing 2nd at Nationals in Sports and Entertainment Marketing), played volleyball, and did student government.

Now, I attend Brigham Young University as a pre-business student. I plan on applying to the Information Systems program. I love the Gospel of Jesus Christ, and I am currently preparing to begin a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints on May 30th.

Why I am the person I am

From a young age, I was taught the Gospel of Jesus Christ. I am so grateful that I was raised this way. Because I was raised in the Church, I try my best to be a Christlike individual. My parents raised us with love, and I will always be grateful for my dad teaching me to always open the door for others. It's the little things that make me the person I am today.
