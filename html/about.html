My main hobby: Waterskiing


Growing up, I drove out to Utah every summer from my home town of Naperville, Illinois. My grandpa, being a waterski purist, made sure that each of his grandkids learned how to waterski. Now, there's no other way I'd rather spend my time.

Volleyball

I started playing volleyball when I was in middle school. I played on my freshman team in Illinois, and then everything changed when I moved to Utah. Somehow I got way better that year after club volleyball, and I got to play on the varsity team with my brother! That year, we went 25-0, won the State Championship, and were both selected to the All-State team.


I kept playing. I played club volleyball junior year, and played on varsity junior year, but our season was cut short due to COVID. I played Senior year, and we lost in the State tournament. I was planning on trying out for the BYU men's team to try to get on the practice squad, but instead hurt my leg and had to get surgery.
